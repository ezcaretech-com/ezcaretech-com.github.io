ezConsent for Royal Commission

May 07, 2024 • 1 min read • RCJY

Provided by Royal Commission For Jubail And Yanbu

Introduce

e-Consent system provides convenient functions for signing consents using desktop computers or tablet computers.
Various medical forms are displayed electronically and signature data are saved in electronic data as well.
This will reduce time in searching and getting medical forms, as well as increasing work efficiency.
